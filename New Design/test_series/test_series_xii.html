TestQ CBSE XII

A Test Series (Dry Run) before you take on the actual school examinations. One of the features of this Test Series is the freedom to take the tests on their own preferred timings and day. As every school has their own sequence of teaching topics in any subject. This feature enables him to take the test of that particular topic which has been recently covered in his respective school. A student can schedule a test 24*7*365 days.

Mega Test Series includes

  • Chapter Tests of all topics
  • 4 no. of Module Tests
  • 5 no. of Full Tests

Click on the link to know the detailed Test Programme of each subject.

Mini Test Series includes

  • 5 No of Full Tests

Click on the link to know the detailed Test Programme of each subject.

To start giving the practice tests, take these simple Steps.

Your Move

  • Step 1 (Choose Package): Choose one of the package consisting of TestQ CBSE from the boxes given below.
  • Step 2 (Subject Selection and Fees): Know your fees from the chosen box in case you want to give tests for all the four subjects (i.e Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Biology & English), otherwise click on "For Individual subjects" and select the combination of subjects from the pop-up.
  • Step 3 (Make Payment): Click on "Pay Now" and proceed for making online payment. Ypu can pay either through Payment Gateway or you can transfer through NEFT.

Live CBSE's Move

Step 1 (Courier Dispatch):

  • As soon as the payment is realized on our end, we will mail you fee receipt, enrolment form link and the test schedule of the course choosen by you.
  • We eill dispatch you 'Conquer CBSE' Course Material (only in case your package consists of study material as well).
  • Step 3 (Make Payment): Click on "Pay Now" and proceed for making online payment. Ypu can pay either through Payment Gateway or you can transfer through NEFT.

Your Move - When you receive test schedule, mail us the date, time and test no. when you want to appear for test along with the topic/topics name.

Step 3 (Getting Started):

  • On your schedule date & time, a mail will be sent to you which will be containing the link of your test paper. Click on the link and start taking the tests.

Step 1 - (Schedule a Test)

  • Whenever you feel you have completely learnt a topic and want to test yourself, schedule a test specifying your preferred time.

Step 2 - (Test Commences)

  • The test paper will be accessible to you online 10 minutes before the scheduled time. Start answering on the answer sheets. The link will get deactivated after the scheduled duration.

Step 3 - (Send Your Answer Sheets)

  • Scan the answer sheets and e-mail the scanned answer sheets to answer@livecbse.com OR courier us at 472-A, Talwandi, Near Modern School, Kota, Rajasthan, Pincode - 324005.

Step 4 - (Checked Answer Sheet)

  • Once your answer sheet is checked, we will e-mail you the question paper and checked answer sheet.

A student can schedule a test anytime on any day, he/she can schedule a test even at mid-night or on Public Holiday. Student just need to inform atleast 24 hrs before the time when he/she wants to appear for Test.

Can I have Test Series for only one subject?

  • Yes we give the option of choosing only 1 or 2 or 3 subjects. Please click on "For Individual Subjects" at the bottom of the packages box listed below.

Who will check the answer sheets?

  • Our experienced teachers having years of experience in CBSE pattern shall check the copies.

Can I buy Conquer CBSE (Study Material) or DoubtFire (Doubt Removal) with this the test series?

  • Yes, you can. See below for possible combinations.

I don't have a scanner and sending individual answer sheets is very expensive. Are there any other alternatives?

  • Ideally scanning and mailing is the best option since its free and fastest. But in case you don't have or can't borrow a scanner, then you can send Answer sheets of 5 tests together rather than sending them separately, which will cut down your cost considerably.

How do you check whether the student has given the test without any malpractices?

  • No, we trust the student that he will not indulge in any malpractice since these are just practice tests before the real test. Additionally we will send sms and mails to the parents before the tests so that they can monitor their child during the practice tests.

Why are you sending the blank answer sheets? Can't I write the answers in any loose sheets?

  • We have designed the answer sheets on the actual answer sheets provided during the CBSE examinations. This is done so as to give the students the practice of writing on that format of paper.

Why is Mini Test Series available after 30th November 2015?

  • The Mini Test series contains Full Tests which covers the entire syllabus. So the Mini Test Series starts only after November.

Can I discuss my corrected answer sheets with the teachers?

  • No, but if you add DoubtFire to your TestQ CBSE then you can discuss your corrected answer sheets with the teachers in your allotted time.

Will I get the solutions/Answers after the test?

  • Yes you will get a "Answer Key" but only after you have e-mailed or couriered your answer sheets.
