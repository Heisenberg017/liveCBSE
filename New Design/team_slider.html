Meet Our Team

The team of faculty members at domain E is constituted by the qualified people of the respective subjects, trained by the core team of domain E. This team is spearheaded by Mr. Abhishek Jain, (B.Tech IIT-Delhi) to monitor the quality of deliverance and development of the pupils. The faculty is accountable for-

  • (a). Elucidating the concepts in their most basic form and exposing the students to all the dimensions of the subject
  • (b). Managing the levels of deliverance in the lecture as per the requirements of the students.
  • (c). Exposing the students with the problems of all levels in the subject so as to stretch the understanding and develop logic of the students.
  • (d). Making the lectures interactive to facilitate the exchange of relevant ideas to help the students clarify their thought process without requiring them to mug up anything without understanding. For supporting students in problem solving and nailing the 'confidence of ability' in them.
