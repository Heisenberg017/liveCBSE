Teaching Methodology At Domain E

At the core of domain E is a team of Professionals, zealously guarding the interests of students aspiring for the various entrance examinations like IIT-JEE, NEET and other state level entrance exams for admission to engineering and medical colleges. At domain E the only pursuit is guiding the students and enabling them to achieve the best ranks in such entrances.

The high quality of deliverance is maintained by recruiting qualified teachers and training them in a manner so that they can empower the students with the skills and knowledge. Such training brings out an achiever in the student and motivates them to secure best possible ranks as per their individual potential. For this domain E interacts with the students on following platforms-

  • 1. Course material
  • 2. Testing and evaluation

1. Course material

No Theory deliverance is considered complete at domain E without a set of appropriate questions on the topic.

Assignments Sheets

In assignments sheet the questions are so designed to pinpoint the relevance of theory in solving problems. These sheets are also a powerful medium keeping students abreast with the topics taught previously, as these may contain questions that require fundamentals of previous topic also.

Tutorial Sheet

This is a carefully formulated set of problems (broken in levels) specific to a particular topic. This contains a set of all possible format problems possible for a particular topic.

2. Testing and evaluation

Tests are an important part of teaching methodology as they help the students to find out the effects of his/her preparation and understanding. Besides this, the tests help the students to build up their temperament for examinations. Simulated tests help the students to conquer examination anxiety and makes them more confident on the examination day.
