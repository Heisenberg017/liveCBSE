Conquer CBSE XI

The Study Material named 'Conquer CBSE' has been designed to facilitate the students who want to stay focussed on their Board Examination once they start preparing for it.

  • Conquer CBSE will help the students to round up their preparations in minimum time with maximum output.
  • Conquer CBSE is precise in the sense that it contains nothing that is unnecessary and nothing important has been left out. The perspective has been the flavor of CBSE Board Examination, and the NCERT text book, yet it is easy to understand.
  • The problem sets are optimized to cover the complete spectrum of problems on a topic that could be a part of CBSE Board Exam.
  • All unnecessary information has been filtered out. Unlike other conventional books available in the market, Conquer CBSE are the notes of a chapter prepared by experts to achieve maximum in Class XI (CBSE) in minimum time.
  • Efforts have been made by the team in making the Course Material best ever in India in terms of (i) presentation of text for understanding etc. (ii) making it error free (iii) quality of diagrams.

The entire course of each subject has been divided into 4 MODULES each. Each of the four modules of a subject contains chapters that have a common thread and form a substantial chunk of marks as per the distribution of marks followed by NCERT. This will help the student in keeping track of his preparation, in terms of marks, as he finishes the modules one after the other. Each chapter has

  • Lessons
  • Lots of Solved examples (including NCERT)
  • Unsolved Exercise
