DoubtFire CBSE XII

The personal doubt removal process has the secondary status whereas many a times it is the real difference maker. The student may have just hit a block somewhere or the other. This takes away the fizz from the other hard work and motivation of the student. To facilitate the student with the same we have developed a system for the students where they get to clear their doubts then and there itself with best system and teachers dedicated for this sole purpose. The only requirement is a basic internet connection (that now most homes have). This would give you the wand that would make your doubts and confusion disappear in a 'jiffy'.

Getting your Doubts Removed is now very simple and effective.

The number of sessions a student gets to use for

  • Conquer CBSE + DoubtFire - (1 no. of sessions per module)
  • TestQ CBSE + DoubtFire - (2 no. of sessions per subject)

* DoubtFire option is only available to those students who buy our Course material and/or Buy our test series.

  • As soon the student has a question to ask, he just has to login and click on DoubtFire.
  • Write the doubt just the topic as well as subtopic or in a sentence format for E.g. I have doubt in Rotational Motion and in the specific topic rolling.
  • The student selects its preferable timing on the teacher time table.
  • This selection is then automatically intimated to the teacher.
  • The teacher then confirms the time. The student is then sent a link for access to the discussion room for a particular time.
  • The student just has to click the link and then he enters the room where the teacher is already waiting for him and then they freely discuss things.

The process is very simplified and organized.

Note:

  • As soon as the session is scheduled the session is reduced from the remaining sessions of the student.
  • The Doubts can be asked from both NCERT and Conquer CBSE Material.

Why do I need to take Conquer CBSE and/or TestQ CBSE along with DoubtFire? Can't I have a stand- alone DoubtFire sessions?

  • No, we don't have a stand-alone DoubtFire course.

I am interested in DoubtFire. But I am confused between "Conquer CBSE + DoubtFire" and "TestQ CBSE + DoubtFire". Which one will be the best for me?

  • While "Conquer CBSE + DoubtFire" is meant for those students who wants to clarify their doubts from NCERT and our study material , but "TestQ CBSE + DoubtFire" is tailor made course for those who want to have good test practice and discuss their corrected answered sheets with the teachers.

I don't have a scanner and sending individual answer sheets is very expensive. Are there any other alternatives?

  • Ideally scanning and mailing is the best option since its free and fastest. But in case you don't have or can't borrow a scanner, then you can send Answer sheets of 5 tests together rather than sending them separately, which will cut down your cost considerably.

What if the session time is not sufficient?

  • If the session time is extended then the teacher will be taking a call whether the session is getting extended because of getting into other topics or the discussion is extended within the focussed area. If the teacher says the session has not been sufficient the extension could be made.

What if the session breaks because of connectivity issues or something else?

  • If the session is being disconnected due to technical snag at the student's end then the session would be counted. If there is a technical snag at the teacher's end then the session will not be counted.
