About Us

Around 2006, Kota still had students coming to coachings for Competitive Examination, but there was no authentic coaching for Board Exams. This resulted in the students ending up achieving lower than their potential, in the Crucial Board Exams. Hence, domain E was started.

domain E, founded in 2006, was started for Class XII Board Exam Preparation, catering to the students preparing for Competitive Examinations at various institutes of Kota. Since 14 years of its inception, domain E has successfully registered more than 17000 students for Class XII Board Exam Preparation alone. These include primarily offline (Kota centre) and online students (Live Coaching via internet). Today domain E has become the first choice of the students preparing for Board Exams. In past few years we have given the best results including Zonal Toppers. The success can be owed to Professional Management, Well Crafted Study Material, Dedicated Teachers and strong ethics for Education.

domain E into Live Education

Inception of Livecbse: In the year 2014 we started with live coaching with real time interactions via internet for CBSE Board Exam Preparation. Over these years we have shown it has been as effective as our physical coaching. Many students have successfully completed their course for the complete academic session and have also registered for successive sessions. This has given us the confidence that we are on the right track and can fill in the gap of lack of availability of competent teachers for other competitive exams essentially based on NCERT.Hence proving the effectiveness of the model.
